Crystal Collector

    Here's How to play the game.

  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
  • There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  • You win the game by matching your total number to the computer's randomly generated number.
  • You lose the game by going above the computer's number.
  • The value of each crystal is a mystery until clicked.
  • Each time the game starts, the values of the crystals will change.
